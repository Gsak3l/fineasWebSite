About Us

Purpose

The purpose of our team, is the development of the first autonomous, electric, formula-style race car in Greece, in order to participate in the «Formula Student Driverless» competitions.

Our Team

The first Greek student team, that has been created for the above purpose, consists of young people, that are full of passion, willing to help, have lots of imagination and craziness as well. In this difficult attempt, we do not only need people with abilities and knowledge. Necessary elements of this team, are the correct organization and excellent cooperation, among its members.

Formula Student Driverless Competition

Formula SAE is one of the biggest student competitions worldwide. It started in 1980 by the SAE student branch at the University of Texas. Over the years, the competition evolved. In 2010, the category of electrical vehicles was added and in 2017 the category of driverless vehicles.
The aim of the competition is for students to cooperate with each other, to present innovative ideas, to design, construct, develop and finally to compete with small, autonomous, formula-style racing cars.
The competition consists of 2 main categories of events. Static events and Dynamic events, with total score of 1000 points.

Dynamic Events

Skidpad 75 points
Acceleration 75 points
Efficiency 100 points
Trackdrive 250 points

Static Events

Business Plan 75 points
Cost 100 points
Efficiency 100 points
Engineering Design 150 points
Autonomous Design 175 points
